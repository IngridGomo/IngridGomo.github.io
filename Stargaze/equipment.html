WHAT DO I NEED?

BLANKET

Or something else to lay on when you're gazing. A towel, pallet, hammock or just good clothing. Whatever you prefer.

RED FLASHLIGHT

If you need some light to walk around, a flashlight with red light will light up your path without interruoting your night vision.

BINOCULARS

If you want to take a closer look you can bring a pair of binoculars, even though it won't help you see a lot more. If you have more advanced telescopes, these could be cool to bring.
